# Composition
Type composition:

Visual composition:
- Main visual: A simplified human silhouette standing before a wall of floating translucent rectangular panels (representing browser/terminal tabs). The figure gently waves one hand, and the panels on one side are dissolving into dandelion-seed-like particles drifting upward into open sky. The left side is dense and cluttered, the right side opens into clean empty space.
- Layout: Figure positioned slightly left of center, facing right. Dense tab-panels cluster on the left 40%, dissolving particles fill the middle transition zone, and the right 30% is serene open space with subtle gradient. Title text placed in the clean right area or top portion.
- Decorative: Soft geometric grid lines fading in background suggesting digital space. Small terminal cursor icons (▊) scattered among the dissolving particles.

Color scheme:
- Primary: #4A90D9 (calm blue)
- Background: #F0F4F8 (light cool grey) transitioning to #E8F0FE (soft blue-white)

Rendering notes: Clean flat-vector style with smooth edges, no gradients within shapes (flat fills), minimal line work. Geometric simplification of all elements. Crisp edges with subtle shadow for depth separation.

Type notes: Metaphor type — the dissolving tabs represent letting go of information anxiety, the open space represents cognitive relief.

Palette notes: Cool palette — blues, teals, and soft greys create a calm, digital, professional atmosphere that matches the tech-focused content while evoking serenity.
